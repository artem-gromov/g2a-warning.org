G2A Bitcoin Payment Warning

My experience with a failed order and no support response

Incident Date: April 20, 2025

What Happened

I placed an order on G2A.com and chose to pay with Bitcoin. I transferred the exact amount to the wallet address provided during checkout. The transaction was confirmed on the blockchain, yet my order status remains "Waiting for payment."

Blockchain Confirmation

The payment was fully confirmed on the Bitcoin network. You can view the transaction here:

https://blockchair.com/bitcoin/transaction/90f4ade635956e34d344e1e1c92e615b304b7ee3a5adb678b9c550a34913f0df

Communication Attempts

I contacted G2A support several times, as well as their listed payment partner, Zonda (BitBay). Zonda confirmed that the wallet address used is not theirs. G2A, however, has not responded meaningfully or clarified who the actual payment provider was.

Why This Matters

Many users online have reported similar issues with G2A, especially when paying with cryptocurrency. A lack of transparency and customer support creates a significant risk of financial loss. This page exists to warn others and encourage G2A to take responsibility for unresolved transactions.

Share Your Experience

If you've had a similar problem with G2A, feel free to reach out or share your story on Reddit, forums, or your own platform. Transparency helps protect others.
